    About

    We're Tiana's kitchen & event and we've been rendering catering services, ushers and servers for events here in Edo state since 2018. We believe in excellence hence our motto: "making your event excellent". We specialise in working directly with event planners, caterers, ushers, servers, security personnels, DJ & MC to make your event excellent.

    I started this business because I was tired of attending disorganized events and I believe there is a better way to plan and have an excellent event.

    ~C.E.O

    We are looking forward to having our own event center and a restaurant across the country. I'd like you to patronize any of our services; ushers, servers and chef, our contact informations are located in the contact section below. Also check out our instagram handle tianas_events_.
